The Player

The Player can be seen in action here:

http://wellcomelibrary.org/player/b18024130

It is a tool for displaying objects in a digital library. It has many things in common with the Internet Archive BookReader, but it's not just about books - although it's good at them. It's a container for playing any kind of digital content that might be found in a library, via plugins and extensions. The Player on the Wellcome Library site shows artworks, audio, video as well as archives, journals, manuscripts and books.

Some features of the player:

  • Structural information for books, allowing security model to be applied
  • Deep Zoom for images (openseadragon)
  • "Search within" and term highlighting
